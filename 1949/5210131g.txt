Forty—seventh and South Parkway, the “hub” of an area of 350,000 Negroes, remains the spot where all "Bronze—
Ville” must sooner or later meet or pass. The huge South Center block which houses the South Center department

Store, employing upward to 200 Negroes, the Regal Theatre, Savoy Ballroom, U. S. and state offices and 150
offices with highest grade professmnal tenants, was erected in 1928.
